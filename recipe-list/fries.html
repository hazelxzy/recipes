French fries recipe

A plate of french fries

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 2-3 cups of vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Peel the potatoes and rinse them under cold water to remove excess starch.
  2. Cut the potatoes into even-sized strips, about 1/4 to 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Place the cut potatoes in a large bowl of cold water and let them soak for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  4. After soaking, drain the potatoes and pat them dry with paper towels or a clean kitchen towel.
  5. In a large, heavy-bottomed pot or deep fryer, heat the vegetable oil to 325째F (165째C).
  6. Carefully add a handful of potatoes to the hot oil, ensuring they are not overcrowded. Fry them for about 4-5 minutes, or until they are just beginning to turn golden brown.
  7. Use a slotted spoon or a wire mesh skimmer to remove the partially fried potatoes from the oil and transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate or baking sheet. Let them cool for about 10-15 minutes.
  8. Increase the heat of the oil to 375째F (190째C). Once the oil is hot, add the partially fried potatoes back into the oil in batches. Fry for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  9. Once the fries are done, remove them from the oil and transfer them to a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil. Immediately sprinkle them with salt while they are still hot.
  10. Serve the fries hot with your favorite dipping sauce or enjoy them on their own.
